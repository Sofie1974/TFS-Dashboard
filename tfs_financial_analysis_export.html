Observation: A Shift in Spending

143%
Growth in General & Admin Costs

From 2021 to 2023, 'General & Admin' costs grew by 143%, while 'Program' spending grew by 43%.

This shows a shift in resource allocation, which is a common trend as organizations scale up operations to support a larger mission.

A 3-Step Analytical Approach

1. Disaggregate the Data

My first step would be to understand the "why" behind the 143% growth. Is it new staff, new software, or one-time costs? This context is key to understanding the data.

2. Correlate with Impact

Next, I'd correlate this data with public impact metrics. If public mentions also grew, this spending could be seen as a successful investment in operations and comms.

3. Identify Opportunities

Finally, I'd use this data to identify opportunities. This could include a tool/software audit or finding new fundraising leads from the existing vendor list.
